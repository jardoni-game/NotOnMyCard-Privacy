Common Questions

How do I create a game?

Launch the app and tap the plus button to start a new game. Customize your card and invite friends to join.

Can I play with friends remotely?

Yes. NotOnMyCard supports multiplayer gameplay. Create a game and share the code with your friends.

The app is crashing. What should I do?

Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the problem persists, please contact us at the email above with details about what you were doing when the crash occurred.

How do I report a bug?

Email us with a description of the issue, what device you're using, and what iOS version you have. The more detail you provide, the faster we can help.
